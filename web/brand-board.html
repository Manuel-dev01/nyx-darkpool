Trade in the dark.

Nyx lets institutions submit and match orders without revealing price or volume to the public mempool. Commitments are sealed as Poseidon hashes; a zero-knowledge proof attests a valid match; a Soroban contract verifies it on-chain before settling atomically.

01 Essence

Nyx is the Greek primordial of night — the one even Zeus would not cross. The brand carries that authority: unseen, exact, and beyond dispute.

001

Shielded

Price and volume never touch the public mempool. Orders exist only as commitments until a match is proven.

002

Provable

A zero-knowledge proof attests that maker and taker legitimately intersect — no trust, no disclosure, no exceptions.

003

Atomic

A Soroban contract verifies the proof on-chain via Stellar's native BN254 host functions, then swaps assets in one settlement.

02 Logo & Mark

The mark is an eclipse. A faint full ring is the whole order book; one luminous crescent is the single match revealed and proven. Two circles — nothing more.
