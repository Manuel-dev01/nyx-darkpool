01 / WHY

When a large order touches a public mempool, the market sees you coming. Price moves before you fill. Nyx removes the tell. Orders never reveal price or volume — only a commitment, and a proof that the match was honest.

"Liquidity should not cost you your hand of cards."

02 / HOW

Four steps. Nothing revealed.

01
Commit
Poseidon hash

Price and size never leave your client. The order is sealed locally as a cryptographic commitment and that hash is all the network ever sees.

02
Prove
Zero-knowledge circuit

A proof attests that a maker and taker order legitimately intersect at a valid price and volume — without disclosing either side of the trade.

03
Verify
Soroban · BN254

A Soroban contract verifies the proof on-chain using Stellar Protocol 26's native BN254 host functions. No trusted operator stands between maker and taker.

04
Settle
Atomic swap

The asset swap clears atomically in a single settlement — the RWA and USDC change hands together, or not at all.
